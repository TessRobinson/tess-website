I offer one-to-one and group singing tuition for ages of 12 and up as well as conducting a range of singing workshops locally. I cover a range of singing genres, specialising in musical theatre but branching out into pop, jazz and classical styles too.

Teaching can take place at my studio in Bath or I am able to travel around Bath and the surrounding areas to suit you. I can accommodate for complete beginners to singers who are at an advanced level. I offer a range of singing lessons from audition tutoring to singing exams or preparation for gigs and shows.

About Me

Currently studying Music and Drama studies at Bath Spa University. Roles in musical theatre include Eponine in Les Miserables, Mrs Lovett in Sweeney Todd, Nancy in Oliver! and Calamity Jane in Calamity Jane as well as smaller roles in The Wizard of Oz, The Boy Friend, Hairspray and Phantom of the Opera.

I have studied under West End singer and star of Phantom of the Opera Claire-Louise Hammacott, Welsh musical director Carys Davies and most recently Helen Porter.
